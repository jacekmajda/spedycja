OUR SERVICES

Storage

Our warehouse is located near Wroclaw. At a crossroad of international motorway and express roads. Offering end to end storage service for everyone. Loading or unloading trucks, picking, packing products and lot more. From small ecommerce to a big factory who needs extra space. Either short and also long term storage. From one cardboard to hundreds of pallets.

Distribution

Our distribution service provides end-to-end solutions from order fulfillment and pick-and-pack operations to last-mile delivery. Whether you need distribution for retail, wholesale, or e-commerce. Our service is tailored to optimize your operations with flexible and scalable solutions to reduces costs, and improves customer satisfaction.

Shipping

We offers a flexible and cost-effective shipping options Less Than Truckload (LTL) and Full Truckload (FTL) whether you need to send single cardboard or an entire truck. Domestic Transport or International Shipping we will handle everything, from customs clearance to last-mile delivery. We specialize in Road Transport, providing safe and timely delivery.

Express deliveries

Our service is designed for anyone who need fast, reliable, and efficient shipping solutions for time-sensitive packages. Ensuring it reaches its destination as quickly as possible, locally, nationally, or internationally. From urgent documents, critical parts to high-value goods. We offer same day delivery, next day delivery, scheduled express options to suit your specific needs, along with door-to-door service.
